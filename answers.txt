Q1: Why might we consider using Git?

Answer: version control, logging our progress, making code changes 
sustainable and as problem free as possible, helpful for collaboration.

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an Example.

Answer: Data science can be applied to healthcare, where it is used to 
analyze patient data and predict disease outcomes. For example, machine 
learning algorithms can help identify high-risk patients by analyzing 
medical records and health metrics.
